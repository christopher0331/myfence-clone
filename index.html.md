# MyFence.com - Seattle Cedar Fence Installation

**Professional cedar fence installation serving the Greater Seattle area with 30+ years of experience**

## About MyFence.com

MyFence.com is a father-son owned fence installation company based in Maple Valley, Washington. We specialize in premium cedar fence installation, serving a 28-mile radius around Maple Valley including Seattle, Bellevue, Issaquah, and surrounding areas.

### What We Install

- **Cedar Fences**: Premium Western Red Cedar privacy fences with multiple styles
- **Hogwire Fences**: Black and galvanized hogwire panels with cedar frames
- **Hybrid Aluminum Systems**: Black aluminum panels with cedar frame and pressure-treated posts

We do NOT install vinyl, composite, ornamental iron, or standalone steel/aluminum fencing.
